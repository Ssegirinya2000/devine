Message from Our Head Teacher

Welcome to Divine Junior School, where we believe every child is uniquely gifted and deserves an education that nurtures their full potential. Our mission is to provide quality education rooted in Christian values that prepares children for academic success and responsible citizenship.

With over 15 years of experience in education, I am proud to lead a team of dedicated teachers who are passionate about shaping young minds. We create a supportive environment where children feel valued, challenged, and inspired to excel.

I invite you to visit our campus and experience the Divine Junior difference firsthand.

Our History

Founded in 2005

Divine Junior School was established in 2005 with a vision to provide quality education to children in Masaka City and surrounding areas. What began as a small pre-primary school with just 25 students has grown into a thriving educational institution serving over 500 students from daycare through primary seven.

Our founders, a group of passionate educators and community leaders, recognized the need for a school that would not only focus on academic excellence but also instill strong moral values and life skills in students.

Over the years, we have expanded our facilities, curriculum, and teaching staff while maintaining our commitment to personalized attention and holistic development. Our graduates have gone on to excel in secondary schools across Uganda and beyond.
