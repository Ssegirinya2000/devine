Building Strong Foundations

Our curriculum emphasizes foundational skills in mathematics and literacy

Early Numeracy Development

At Divine Junior School, we believe in building strong mathematical foundations from an early age. Our approach to teaching numbers and counting ensures that children develop not just rote memorization, but true understanding of mathematical concepts.

Through hands-on activities, games, and practical applications, we make learning numbers engaging and meaningful for our young learners.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Number recognition and counting (1-100+)
  • Basic arithmetic operations
  • Problem-solving skills
  • Mathematical vocabulary development
  • Real-world application of mathematical concepts

Admission Requirements

Day Care & Pre-Primary

  • Birth Certificate (Copy)
  • 2 Passport-size photographs
  • Medical immunization card
  • Completed application form
  • Previous school report (if applicable)

Primary Section

  • Birth Certificate (Copy)
  • 3 Passport-size photographs
  • Previous school reports (2 years)
  • Transfer letter from previous school
  • Completed application form
  • Interview with pupil & parents

Admission Process

  1. Collect and complete the application form from school office or download from website
  2. Submit completed form with required documents
  3. Attend an interview with the Head Teacher (for Primary section)
  4. Receive admission decision within 5 working days
  5. Pay required fees to secure placement
  6. Attend orientation before term begins
