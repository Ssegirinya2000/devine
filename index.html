About Our School

Divine Junior School is a premier educational institution located in Masaka City, Bulayi, Bukakata Road. Founded in 2005, we have established ourselves as a leading primary school dedicated to holistic child development.

Our school provides a nurturing environment where children are encouraged to explore their talents, develop critical thinking skills, and grow in Christian values. We offer a comprehensive curriculum that meets the Ugandan National Standards while incorporating modern teaching methodologies.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a team of dedicated, qualified teachers, we ensure each child receives personalized attention to reach their full potential.

Our Educational Levels

We provide a comprehensive educational journey from early childhood through primary education

Day Care

Our Day Care program welcomes children from 1-3 years old, providing a safe, stimulating environment for early development. We focus on sensory play, basic social skills, and foundational learning through age-appropriate activities.

Pre-Primary

For children aged 3-6 years, our Pre-Primary program introduces structured learning while maintaining play-based activities. We focus on literacy, numeracy, creative expression, and social development to prepare children for primary education.

Primary

Our Primary section (P1-P7) follows the Ugandan curriculum with emphasis on academic excellence, character formation, and holistic development. We prepare students for the Primary Leaving Examinations (PLE) while nurturing their unique talents.
