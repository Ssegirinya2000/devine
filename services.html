Science & Environmental Education

Comprehensive Science Curriculum

At Divine Junior School, we provide a robust science education that introduces students to various scientific concepts including vectors, biology, and environmental science.

Key Science Topics:

  • Vectors & Mathematics: Introduction to basic mathematical concepts
  • Biology & Health: Study of living organisms including mosquito and tick biology
  • Environmental Science: Understanding ecosystems and conservation
  • Practical Experiments: Hands-on laboratory activities
  • Health Education: Disease prevention and personal hygiene

Our science program prepares students for advanced scientific studies while fostering curiosity and critical thinking about the natural world.

Monthly Educational Activities

February Learning Focus

Each month at Divine Junior School features special educational themes and activities. In February, our focus includes:

February Activities:

  • Mathematics reinforcement and assessment
  • Science fair preparations
  • Cultural exchange programs
  • Health and hygiene awareness
  • Environmental conservation projects
  • Parent-teacher conferences

Our monthly themes ensure comprehensive coverage of all curriculum areas while keeping learning engaging and relevant to current events and seasons.

Our School Uniform

Smart, comfortable uniforms that promote identity and unity among our students

Boys' Uniform

Our boys wear navy blue trousers/shorts with a light blue shirt. The uniform includes a school tie with the school crest, black shoes, and white socks. For sports, boys wear blue shorts and a white t-shirt with the school logo.

Girls' Uniform

Our girls wear navy blue skirts/pinafores with a light blue blouse. The uniform includes a school tie with the school crest, black shoes, and white socks. For sports, girls wear blue shorts/skirts and a white t-shirt with the school logo.
